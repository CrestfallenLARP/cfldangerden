- **EMERGENCY:** 
- **Bathrooms**: Claddagh Hall has a male, female, and handicap accessible bathroom available inside. There are also additional rustic restrooms found near the adirondack style cabins.
-  **Showers**: If you are interested in shower facilities, please inform us of this in your registration. The shower house is a rustic building and is weatherized unless we request its use.
- **Smoking**: Smoking is only permitted in parking areas, cement pads, or stone pathways. Put cigarette butts in trays or cans. Do not smoke indoors, including vaping. We are willing to ban smoking if any cigarette butts are found. Please be courteous.
- **Sleeping**: Claddagh Hall will be sectioned off to have sleeping on one side and the meeting hall on the other. Adirondack and tent camping will also be available.
- **Camp Boundaries**: When arriving on site, take the left hand gravel road to find the main parking lot. The building on the left and two buildings directly ahead of the parking lot are residences, please do not disturb. To the left is a large pond with a pavilion that we may use. On the right up the hill is Claddagh Hall which is used for check in and the meeting hall.  Behind Claddagh Hall there is a large path through the woods holding a number of tent camping platforms, aiderondack style cabins, a shower house, fields, and more.
- **Cell Phone Service**: Low, there is limited service from the parking area and main paths.
